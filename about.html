About Samuwa EducaNews

Samuwa EducaNews is a trusted platform for Kenyan students and educators. We provide accessible, accurate, and diverse learning materials—from notes and past papers to videos and news—tailored for exam success and lifelong learning.

🎯 Our Mission

To democratize education by providing free and premium educational resources to all learners in Kenya and beyond.

🌟 Our Vision

To become the leading online hub for academic success and educational empowerment across East Africa.

🧭 Our Core Values

  • 💡 Knowledge Empowerment: We provide accurate and useful learning tools to empower every learner.
  • 📚 Accessibility for All: We ensure educational resources are within reach for every student, anytime, anywhere.
  • 🌍 Integrity & Accuracy: We publish only well-researched, relevant, and verified content.
  • 🚀 Innovation in Learning: We embrace modern tools to simplify and enhance education.
  • 🤝 Community-Driven: We listen to and grow with our learners, parents, and teachers.
  • 🎯 Focus on Impact: We aim to improve student outcomes and confidence through effective resources.
  • 🛡️ Privacy and Safety: We protect our users’ data and offer a safe online learning space.
