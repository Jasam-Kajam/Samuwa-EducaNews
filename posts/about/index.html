About Samuwa EducaNews

Our Mission

To empower readers across Kenya and beyond by providing high-quality educational resources, insightful news, inspiring stories, and practical life tips—all in one place.

Our Vision

To be a trusted and engaging digital platform where people come to learn, grow, stay informed, and connect with meaningful content daily.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity – We deliver accurate, honest, and well-researched content.
  • Access – We believe everyone deserves free or affordable access to valuable information and learning.
  • Creativity – We explore unique topics, formats, and storytelling styles to keep content fresh and engaging.
  • Inclusivity – We cover diverse topics that speak to different communities and backgrounds.
  • Empowerment – Our goal is to inspire learning, critical thinking, and personal development in every reader.

Who We Are

Samuwa EducaNews is more than just an education website. It's a growing digital space where you can explore academic resources, trending stories, career tips, study tools, and real-life experiences shared by students, teachers, and everyday people.

We offer:

  • Educational notes, past papers, and revision materials
  • General news and community updates
  • Student and teacher spotlights
  • Video content and guides
  • Motivational and career articles
